# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Discord server members and moderators using the optional web dashboard for fast sound playback, upload moderation, bot controls, and activity review. They are usually in an active voice/chat context and need the interface to respond quickly without hiding the library behind decorative chrome.

## Product Purpose

Discord Brain Rot provides a web control surface for a Discord soundboard bot: browse and play sounds, inspect recent actions, manage favorites, upload or moderate clips, trigger TTS and control-room actions, and review analytics. Success means users can find and fire the right sound quickly while admins can trust status, moderation, and analytics signals.

## Brand Personality

Chaotic premium: playful and server-native, but disciplined enough for a live control desk. The interface should feel like a polished booth for messy audio culture, not a generic SaaS dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS styling, beige gradient hero templates, hero-metric cliches, identical card grids, decorative glassmorphism, and novelty that makes controls harder to trust.

## Design Principles

- Keep playback and bot status first; visual energy must not slow the main task.
- Treat dense tables as a feature, with clear hierarchy and reliable controls.
- Make chaos feel curated through sharp color roles, strong focus states, and consistent component behavior.
- Prefer server-native language and direct labels over marketing copy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, keyboard-friendly controls, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts that preserve playback and moderation actions on mobile.
